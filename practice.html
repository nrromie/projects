My Travel Blog

On July 3rd, I traveled to Sitakunda where I visited Shohosrodhara Waterfall and Bashbaria Sea Beach. I was captivated by the natural beauty of Shohosrodhara to some extent, but unfortunately, the authority there provided a terrible experience. They charged us double the usual price simply because we didn't negotiate. Additionally, we had to wait for over an hour to board the boat, which was also in a very poor condition. However, I thoroughly enjoyed my time at Bashbaria Sea Beach.
